Are Aja Wilson And Josh Cunningham Still Dating?

September 2024 · 4 minute read

A’ja Wilson and Josh Cunningham are professional basketball players. Both of them are well-accomplished and one of the most famous athletes. After they started dating, their relationship made a big headline.

However, their approach of keeping things private has made their fans wonder if they are still dating or not. Thus, in today’s article, let’s find out if the love birds are still together or not, including their love story.

Who Are A’ja and Josh?

  • A’ja currently plays for a WNBA team, Las Vegas Aces, which she joined in 2018.
  • She even led the team to win the 2022 WNBA Championship and was named WNBA MVP.
  • At the same time, Josh plays for KK Borac Bonja Luka, a European Club. Before that, he played for Southland Sharks. 
  • Being basketball players, it might have been easy for the couple to bond.

Met At High School

As reported by Dayton Daily, the couple met back when they were still in high school. They toured the same university, the University of South Carolina. But they ended up attending different colleges.

Josh Cunningham’s girlfriend wanted him to go to the same university and tried to convince him. Later, she went to the University of South Carolina.

Likewise, A’ja Wilson’s boyfriend went to Bradley University in Illinois. But shortly after, he transferred to the University of Dayton in Ohio.

In an interview, A’ja revealed that she reached out to her now-boyfriend not only to consider the school but to know more about him.

Furthermore, the WNBA player admitted that her lover is a man of few words, which created some awkward moments.

Although they joined different colleges, the duo kept in touch with each other. They exchanged messages occasionally and realized that they have a lot in common.

Apparently, the duo’s birthdays are in the same months. A’ja’s is on August 8, while Josh’s is on August 11. Finally, in 2018, the love birds started dating and have been together ever since.

Both A’ja and her lover are doing well in their career. In addition, the duo earns a lot of money. As an active athlete, the Dayton University graduate seems to have earned a hefty sum in his career.

So, Aja Wilson’s boyfriend must be earning within the stated range. As a result, Josh Cunningham’s net worth must be a significant sum.

Furthermore, the loving boyfriend also promotes several clothing brands on his Instagram account. Thus, his earnings must also benefit from those endorsement deals.

In addition, A’ja Wilson’s beau also has a YouTube channel that he runs with his friend, Jermaine D Morgan. But they have not uploaded any video since January 2021.

On the other hand, Spotrac reported that the South Carolina graduate signed 2 years of a contract worth $398,422 with the Las Vegas Aces.

In 2022, Josh Cunningham’s girlfriend is expected to earn an average of $196,267 while carrying a cap hit of $196,267.

Even during her rookie contract, Wilson had an average pay of $58,045 per season. Now, with her new deal, her fortune is bound to increase drastically.

Likewise, the Las Vegas Ace player has a huge fan following on her social media. Owing to her fame, she has inked a lucrative endorsement deal with Mountain Dew.

The deals will certainly benefit A’ja Wilson’s earnings. Considering their endeavors and earnings, there is no doubt that the pair are living comfortable lives.

Are The Duo Still Dating?

It remains a mystery whether or not A’ja Wilson and Josh Cunningham are still together. The couple prefers to keep their relationship details to themselves.

Furthermore, the duo has not posted any pictures together on their social media accounts. Both of them are active on Instagram and regularly update only about their professional life.

In addition, the athletes have not followed one another on Instagram, which is taken as a sign of breakup in today’s world.

Josh Cunningham’s girlfriend once mentioned that they survive long-distance relationships by Face Timing and texting a lot.

But with the recent developments of them not following one another on social media, could it be the duo has separated?

Nonetheless, we hope to learn the truth about their relationship soon. We look forward to hearing more about their dating life.
