Winston Duke Wiki/Biography

Born on 15 November 1986, Winston Duke’s age is 36 Years Old as of 2022. His birthplace was Argyle, in Saint Paul, Trinidad and Tobago. There he was raised and spent his early childhood days till the age of 9. He relocated to Rochester, New York. He holds Trinidadian nationality and he has belief in Christian religion.

Winston Duke Childhood Pic

Winston had his early education at a local school in Argyle, back in his native land. Then when he immigrated to the USA alongside his mom and sister, he started school at Brighton High School which is situated in Rochester, New York. He completed his high school graduation in 2004.

Then he started his Bachelor of Arts degree in Theatre Studies at the University of Buffalo (situated in Amherst, New York). After completing his BA in Theatre he enrolled at Yale University’s Yale School of Drama. There he started studying acting. He completed the Master of Fine Arts (MFA) degree in acting from there in the year 2013.
