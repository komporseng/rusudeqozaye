All My Life Lyrics

May 2024 · 5 minute read

“All My Life” is a track by American rapper Lil Durk, featuring vocals from American rapper J. Cole.

Released on September 23, 2022, it serves as the lead single from Durk’s ninth studio album, Almost Healed.

“All My Life Lyrics” were written by Lil Durk, J. Cole, Dr. Luke, Rocco Did It Again!, and Theron Thomas.

The song was produced by Wheezy and Chopsquad DJ.

“All My Life” is a trap tune with a catchy chorus.

Its  All My Life Lyrics reflect Durk’s upbringing in Chicago and his path to success.

The production, Durk’s vocals, and J. Cole’s feature have received praise.

The track has achieved commercial success, peaking at number 17 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

Additionally, it has been certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).
