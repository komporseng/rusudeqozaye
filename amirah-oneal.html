Amirah O'Neal is the daughter of NBA legendary player Shaquille O'Neal. Shaquille is one of the most famous players to come out of the NBA. Amirah is also a basketball player like her father.

Amirah was born on November 13, 2001, in Santa Monica, California, the United States of America to parents Shaquille O'Neal and Shaunie O'Neal as Amirah Sanaa O'Neal. She has five siblings, including two from her father's and mother's other relationship. She studies at the Crossroads School and plays for their team roadrunners.

Relationship & Affairs

The 19 years old is currently not in any relationship but rather pursuing her dream to be a player like her father. Since then, she is not involved in a relationship; let's talk about her parents.

Amirah's parents got married on December 26, 2002, at the prestigious Beverly Hills Hotel in Beverly Hills, California. They were married for seven years. The couple was already dating for a considerable amount of time before deciding to tie the knot. 

The couple has four children together two before their marriage and two after it. Shareef O'Neal is the eldest born in 2000, and Amirah was born the year after. After their marriage, Shaqir O'Neal was born in 2003, and Me'arah O'Neal was born in 2006.

Amirah's parents were separated on September 4, 2007, but later reconciled. The couple would then divorce eventually when Shaunie filed for divorce on November 10, 2009, citing "irreconcilable differences."

Before their marriage, Amirah's parents each had one child from the previous relationship. Shaq had a daughter Taahirah and Shaunie had a son Myles.

Basketball Career

Amirah is trying to follow in her father's footsteps on becoming a pro player. Shaq was the first overall pick in the NBA draft of 1992 by Orlando Magic. His four years with them saw him bag $17 million from 1992-1996.
