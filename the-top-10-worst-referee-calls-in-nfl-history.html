1. Fail Mary - Seahawks vs. Packers (September 24, 2012)

One of the most infamous referee calls in NFL history occurred during a game between the Seattle Seahawks and the Green Bay Packers. With replacement officials working due to a labor dispute, a Hail Mary pass led to a contentious decision. Impact: The controversial call directly impacted the game's outcome, resulting in a 14-12 victory for the Seahawks. The call ignited widespread debate and scrutiny, prompting the NFL to take swift action to address the issue.

Impact: The missed pass interference call altered the course of the game, leading to the Rams' victory and sparking widespread outrage. The incident prompted the NFL to implement a one-year rule change, allowing pass interference to be reviewed by replay for the subsequent season.
