The 2022 Pro Bowl catches lots of excitement from NFL fans annually. This year’s event is going to thrill lovers of the game as usual. It will be happening on February 6, 2022.

This article will touch on exceptional star Kirk Cousins who has been influential in the game. Kirk Cousins is a professional football player from the United States.

He was picked by the Washington Redskins as the 102nd pick in the 2012 NFL Draft and has spent his whole NFL career with the Redskins.

Cousins was selected NFL Pepsi Rookie of the Week and Fedex Clutch Player of the Week in 2012. In 2015, he led the NFL in completion % and was named NFC Offensive Player of the Week twice.

He also established a Redskins season yardage record with 4,166 yards and was awarded NFC Offensive Player of the Month in December 2015. He played in the 2017 Pro Bowl.

He is 6 feet 3 inches  (1.91 meters or 191 centimeters)  and weighs 93 kg
