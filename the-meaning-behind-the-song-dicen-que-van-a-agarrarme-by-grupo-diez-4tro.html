The Meaning Behind The Song: Dicen Que Van A Agarrarme by Grupo Diez 4tro

June 2024 · 3 minute read
TitleDicen Que Van A Agarrarme
ArtistGrupo Diez 4tro
Writer/ComposerJesus Moreno
AlbumConsejos De La Vida (2022)
Release DateMay 27, 2022
GenreRegional Mexicano, Corrido, Latin Pop, Latin Urban
ProducerGrupo Diez 4tro

“Dicen Que Van A Agarrarme” is a song by Grupo Diez 4tro, written by Jesus Moreno. It is featured in their album “Consejos De La Vida” released on May 27, 2022. The song falls under the genres of Regional Mexicano, Corrido, Latin Pop, and Latin Urban. The group, Grupo Diez 4tro, produced the song themselves.

The lyrics of “Dicen Que Van A Agarrarme” reflect the experiences of Grupo Diez 4tro and their journey from humble beginnings to success. The song speaks about the challenges they faced, the betrayals they encountered, and the triumphs they achieved despite the obstacles in their path.

The opening lines, “¿Qué van a agarrarme? / Pues vengan a calarle” set the tone for the song. Grupo Diez 4tro confidently addresses those who doubt their abilities and promises to prove them wrong. They embrace their position as underdogs and acknowledge that they have seen it all, nothing can scare them anymore.

The verses emphasize the hard work and determination it took for Grupo Diez 4tro to rise above their circumstances. They highlight the importance of respecting the word and not paying heed to those who talk without accomplishments of their own or those who may be envious.

The chorus states, “No confían en la familia, que hasta en la mía existe la envidia / Esto sí es un buen ejemplo de que mejor solo que acompañado.” Here, the artist reflects on the unfortunate reality of envy even within their own family. They express that it is sometimes better to be alone and self-reliant rather than being accompanied by those who don’t believe in you.

As for my personal experience with this song, I resonate with Grupo Diez 4tro’s message of overcoming adversity and not letting negativity bring you down. This song serves as a reminder to stay focused on your goals, even when others doubt you or try to bring you down.

I believe we all face moments in our lives where people may doubt our abilities or try to undermine our success. It is during these times that we need to trust ourselves and continue working towards our aspirations. Grupo Diez 4tro’s lyrics inspire resilience and determination, reminding us that achieving our dreams is possible despite any obstacles that may come our way.

The song also highlights the importance of surrounding ourselves with genuine and supportive individuals. It cautions against putting trust in those who may betray us or act out of envy. This serves as a reminder to choose our company wisely and prioritize relationships built on trust, loyalty, and mutual support.

In conclusion, “Dicen Que Van A Agarrarme” by Grupo Diez 4tro is a powerful song that speaks to the struggles, triumphs, and mindset needed to overcome obstacles. It encourages listeners to stay focused, believe in themselves, and not let the negativity of others hinder their progress. This song serves as a reminder that perseverance and determination can lead to success, even in the face of adversity.

So, the next time you listen to “Dicen Que Van A Agarrarme,” remember the message it carries and let it inspire you to keep pushing forward, no matter what challenges may come your way.
