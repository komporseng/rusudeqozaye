Adams’ father’s name is Czesio Adams. He and his wife both worked long hours in factories. As a result, Adams and her siblings were not raised in a wealthy family. They did, however, learn the value of hard work from their parents. Her siblings’ names are Sylvia Wrobel and Adamski.
