Pamela Reif Workout And Weight Loss

At first, Pamela Reif didn't know about workouts in the gym. She starts to went to the gym with friends. Unfortunately, Pamela didn’t get the training plan she’d expected.

Pamela Reif with her workout routine in the gym as seen on her Instagram post on 28 April 2020 (©: Instagram/Pamela Reif)

The plan Pamela constantly follows countless cardio sessions, along with some light weightlifting and group training classes, which have helped in weight loss. Training for weeks, she has no change in her physique, and she loses her weight. She said:

"During this time my body got very lean, my body fat percentage was pretty low too, my abs were strong & visible but I was extremely skinny. I weighed about 45kg.”

Pamela eventually changes her way of training. She changes into weightlifting on her routine and consisting of squats, deadlifts, and other compound exercises. Combined with her nutrient-rich diet, Pamela’s new training program change her physique drastically. With her fitness journey, Pamela started to record and upload a video on YouTube.

Pamela Reif uploads her daily workout video on YouTube. Pamela has collaborated with so many of her workout celebrities such as Jason Derulo, Selena Gomez, and others.

Pamela's workout consists of a 10-minutes glute routine, which is as follow:

• Pulse lunge, 30 seconds non-stop each leg

• Lunge with leg raise, 30 seconds non-stop each leg

• Jump squats non-stop for 30 seconds

• Side Squat Steps, 30 seconds non-stop

• Sumo Squat, 30 seconds non-stop

• Abductor Squat, 30 seconds non-stop

• Donkey Kicks, 30 seconds non-stop each leg

• Fire Hydrants, Straight Kick-back, 30 seconds non-stop each leg
